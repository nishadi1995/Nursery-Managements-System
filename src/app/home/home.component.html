ABOUT Us

Explore us!!

"singithi Kids Paradice" is a early childhood development center which prepares children for school education.We conduct a one year program which covers almost all the areas that a child should lear.Plus we have a qualified staff to teach to students and also there is a minor staff to take care of the child.Our program contains educational trips too.

We conduct the program every weekday from 8 to 12.We are usually closed on poya days and other special holidays.Contact us for more info.
